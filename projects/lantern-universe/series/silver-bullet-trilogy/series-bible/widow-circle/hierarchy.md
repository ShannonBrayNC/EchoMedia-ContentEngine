# Widow Circle Hierarchy

**Sprint:** SB-002  
**Status:** Active fictional antagonist design  
**Series:** Silver Bullet Trilogy

## Purpose
Define a fictional hierarchy that gives the Widow Circle story texture without turning the group into a real-world operational model.

## Design Rule
The hierarchy should feel social, emotional, and symbolic. It should not feel like a corporate org chart or tactical command structure.

## Story Layers

## The Center
The ideological core. These are the people who shape the Circle's language, rituals, and interpretation of events.

### Narrative Function
They preserve doctrine and decide what the group believes a story means.

### Horror Function
They do not need to be loud. Their authority comes from calm certainty.

## The Voices
The socially active members. They repeat the Circle's preferred framing and make uncertainty feel socially unsafe.

### Narrative Function
They spread atmosphere and pressure through conversation, suggestion, and selective concern.

### Horror Function
They make Jack feel like the story is everywhere before anyone openly confronts him.

## The Witnessed
People who accept the Circle's version of events without fully joining it.

### Narrative Function
They show how narrative corruption spreads beyond committed members.

### Horror Function
They reveal that the Circle does not need everyone to join. It only needs people to behave cautiously around the false story.

## The Doubters
Members or near-members who sense contradiction but fear social cost.

### Narrative Function
They create tension, possible leaks, and moral conflict.

### Horror Function
Their silence shows how fear protects the corrupted narrative.

## The Absent
People who are not physically present but whose stories, wounds, or memories are invoked to justify the Circle's certainty.

### Narrative Function
They give the Circle mythology and emotional authority.

### Horror Function
The dead, missing, or wounded become symbols used by the living.

## Rewrite Guidance
Use the hierarchy to create layered pressure:

- The Center defines meaning.
- The Voices normalize meaning.
- The Witnessed act on meaning.
- The Doubters reveal moral tension.
- The Absent provide mythology.

## Producer Review Note
This hierarchy is for fictional narrative design. It should not include real-world organizing instructions, recruitment instructions, or tactical guidance.
