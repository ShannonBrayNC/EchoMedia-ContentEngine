# Book 2 Title Candidates

**Sprint:** SB-008  
**Series:** Silver Bullet Trilogy  
**Selected Title:** The Echo Ledger

## Selection Criteria
Book 2 title needed to communicate:

- expansion beyond Jack's individual case
- records and preserved fragments
- repeated harm across multiple people
- social echo and narrative distortion
- a darker investigative/witness function
- continuity with Silver Bullet without sounding like a generic sequel

## Candidate List

| Rank | Title | Assessment |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | **The Echo Ledger** | Selected. Combines social echo with record/accounting imagery. Suggests prior entries, hidden records, and accumulated harm. |
| 2 | The Echo Pattern | Strong conceptually, but more abstract and less novelistic. |
| 3 | The Second Shot | Clear sequel energy, but too action-oriented and less tied to records/witness themes. |
| 4 | The Witness Ledger | Strong mythology alignment, but gives away too much Living Anchor direction too early. |
| 5 | The Pattern of Lies | Direct but too generic. |
| 6 | Echo Chamber | Recognizable phrase, but too common and too tied to internet/social-media discourse. |
| 7 | The Ledger of Ruin | Strong gothic tone, but too melodramatic for grounded psychological horror. |
| 8 | The Record of Echoes | Good imagery, but less sharp than The Echo Ledger. |

## Locked Title
# The Echo Ledger

## Rationale
The title works because Book 2 is not merely about another attack. It is about discovering that prior attacks left entries: fragments, records, witness notes, altered timelines, missing context, and people who were reduced to stories they could not correct.
