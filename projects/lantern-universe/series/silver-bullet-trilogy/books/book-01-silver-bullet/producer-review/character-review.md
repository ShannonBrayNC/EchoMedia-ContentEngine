## Character Strengths

- Jack's technical competence is central to the witness arc.
- Supporting characters are not reduced to simple villains.
- The manuscript distinguishes fear, caution, harm, and malice.
- Witness behavior is shown as costly and partial.
- Jack's public double is a strong identity-horror device.

## Character Notes for Pass 2

1. Add at least one early scene showing Marcus and Jack's prior trust before the loss lands.
2. Give Kendra a clearer personal reason to be cautious.
3. Give Evelyn at least one embodied scene, or one scene where her social presence is felt more directly.
4. Clarify Lena's relationship to Jack before the soup scene.
5. Make Mara Venn feel earned rather than merely useful at the end.
6. Maintain Jack's agency; do not let trauma turn him passive.

## Relationship Continuity Notes

- Kendra: distance to doubt to witness thread.
- Lena: care to harm-by-concern.
