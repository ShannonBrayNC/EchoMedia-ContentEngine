# Action Items Before Public-Facing Use

**Sprint:** SB-012  
**Book:** Book 01 — Silver Bullet  
**Reviewed Draft:** SB-011 Pass 2

## Purpose
List the required actions before any public excerpt, marketing, packaging, submission, or release.

## Required Action Items

## 1. Evelyn Embodiment and Composite Distinction
**Status:** required

Create a focused pass that makes Evelyn Blackwood more distinctly fictional through invented biography, social context, motive architecture, and scene presence.

Deliverables:

- Evelyn composite profile refresh
- antagonist embodiment notes
- revised scene targets
- Producer Review note

## 2. Name / Entity / Place Scan
**Status:** required

Scan the manuscript for all names, places, institutions, projects, and artifacts.

Check for:

- accidental real-world resemblance
- inconsistent fictional labels
- identifiable geography
- real institution echoes
- unsupported entity drift

## 3. Correspondence-Language Scan
**Status:** required

Review all messages, notices, summaries, and formal artifacts.

Check for:

- wording too close to real correspondence
- legalistic phrasing that creates real-world mapping risk
- inconsistent artifact naming
- over-specific metadata

## 4. Chronology-Distance Scan
**Status:** required

Confirm the story sequence is sufficiently transformed into fiction.

Check for:

- exact real-world timing echoes
- identifiable event order
- over-specific date/time chains
- scenes that feel documentary rather than fictional

## 5. Public Positioning Draft
**Status:** required

Create fiction-first positioning language for future marketing.

Required framing:

- novel
- fictional psychological thriller / horror
- composite characters
- invented setting
- not memoir
- not true-crime

## 6. Producer Sign-Off
**Status:** required

After all checks are complete, create a final Producer Review sign-off before public-facing use.
