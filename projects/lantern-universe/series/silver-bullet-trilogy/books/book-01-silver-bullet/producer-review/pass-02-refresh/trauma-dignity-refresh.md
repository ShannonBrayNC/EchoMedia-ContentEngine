## Strengths

- Pass 2 better integrates house sounds, body timing, and grounding details.
- Jack remains technically capable and morally precise under pressure.
- The manuscript preserves the distinction between trauma response and unreliability.
- Supporting witnesses are treated as afraid humans, not proof devices.
