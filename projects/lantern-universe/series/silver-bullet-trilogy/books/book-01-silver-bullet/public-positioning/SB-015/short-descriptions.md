## 50-Word Description
In fictional Briar County, technical expert Jack Mercer trusts sequence, records, and systems. But when careful language and social pressure begin reshaping his life, Jack learns that records can preserve truth or bury it. To survive, he must become a witness before the story erases him.

## 100-Word Description
Jack Mercer knows systems. He trusts logs, sequence, and records because they keep the world from becoming noise. But in fictional Briar County, a careful message becomes social pressure, social pressure becomes institutional caution, and incomplete records begin reshaping Jack into a man he does not recognize. As friends step back and formal language closes around him, Jack builds a counter-record to preserve context before the story replaces him. **Silver Bullet** is a fictional psychological thriller about damaged records, fractured reputation, and the human cost of becoming a witness to the truth.
