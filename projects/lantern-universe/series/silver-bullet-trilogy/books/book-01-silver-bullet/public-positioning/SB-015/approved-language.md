## Purpose
Provide safe, fiction-first language for later use after final producer sign-off.

## Approved Core Description
**Silver Bullet** is a fictional psychological thriller set in Briar County, where wounded technical expert Jack Mercer fights to preserve context after social pressure, damaged records, and careful language begin reshaping his life into a story he no longer controls.

## Approved One-Sentence Positioning
A fictional psychological thriller about a technical expert whose life is distorted by missing context, social echo, and the frightening gap between records and truth.

## Approved Short Positioning
In fictional Briar County, Jack Mercer trusts systems because systems keep order. But when careful words, social pressure, and incomplete records begin rewriting his life, he must learn that evidence alone cannot preserve truth. Human witness can.
