# Character Deepening Plan

**Sprint:** SB-006A  
**Book:** Book 01 — Silver Bullet  
**Status:** Active expansion plan

## Purpose
Define how Pass 2 should deepen the supporting cast without creating canon drift or likeness risk.

## Guiding Principle
Every supporting character should feel like a person before they function as plot pressure.

## Priority Characters

## Jack Mercer
### Current Function
Primary protagonist, veteran witness, technical expert, targeted survivor.

### Pass 2 Deepening
- Add more ordinary competence before the rupture.
- Show one non-crisis preference or habit that survives the story pressure.
- Expand body-management routine without over-medicalizing.
- Preserve humor or dry intelligence under pressure.

### Risk
Over-centering suffering can flatten Jack into trauma. Keep skill, wit, stubbornness, and care visible.

## Evelyn Blackwood
### Current Function
Initial framing source and local expression of the wider pressure pattern.

### Pass 2 Deepening
- Add invented biographical specificity that separates her from any real-world analogue.
- Give her a distinct fictional voice and social method.
- Show how she believes she is morally right without validating the harm.
- Keep her frightening through arrangement, implication, and certainty.

### Risk
Do not make Evelyn a one-to-one real-person stand-in. Do not rely on direct real-world correspondence patterns.

## Marcus
### Current Function
Lost ally and false-mercy channel.

### Pass 2 Deepening
- Add an early memory or scene proving prior trust.
- Make his retreat emotionally painful because he has helped before.
- Let his fear feel human, not villainous.
- Clarify why he wants quiet restoration rather than public truth.

### Risk
If Marcus is only weak, the loss becomes flat. Preserve complexity.

## Kendra
### Current Function
Quiet doubter and witness thread.

### Pass 2 Deepening
- Give her a personal reason to fear involvement.
- Show why she notices timing and language patterns.
- Let her courage remain partial.
- Make her witness act specific and costly.

### Risk
Do not turn Kendra suddenly heroic. Her fear is part of the truth environment.

## Lena
### Current Function
Ambiguous care/harm figure through the soup scene.

### Pass 2 Deepening
- Establish why she would bring food.
- Clarify her relationship to Jack before the social pressure changes.
- Keep the soup scene morally complicated.
- Let her believe she is helping.

### Risk
Do not make care automatically false. The power is that care and harm coexist.

## Devin
### Current Function
Information conduit and partial believer.

### Pass 2 Deepening
- Give him a more distinct voice.
- Clarify why he calls Jack with the altered version.
- Make him uncomfortable but not malicious.
- Let him become a bridge to Mara Venn in a grounded way.

### Risk
Avoid making Devin a convenient exposition delivery tool.

## Harlan
### Current Function
Institutional-process figure.

### Pass 2 Deepening
- Keep her professional and plausible.
- Give her institutional incentives rather than villain motives.
- Make her language precise and careful.
- Let her represent system behavior, not personal hatred.

### Risk
Avoid cartoon villainy. Her power comes from process language.

## Mara Venn
### Current Function
First true reader and Book 2 bridge.

### Pass 2 Deepening
- Seed her earlier through Devin or a veteran/support network reference.
- Give her a clear reason to understand sequence.
- Make her cautious and limited, not a rescuer.
- Let her function as a reader of context before becoming a larger Book 2 bridge.

### Risk
Avoid deus ex machina. Mara must be earned.

## Character Expansion Acceptance Test
A character expansion passes if it:

1. Adds individuality.
2. Avoids one-to-one real-world mapping.
3. Strengthens plot pressure or witness continuity.
4. Preserves moral complexity.
5. Does not over-explain canon.

## Producer Decision
Character deepening plan: **approved for Pass 2 planning**.
