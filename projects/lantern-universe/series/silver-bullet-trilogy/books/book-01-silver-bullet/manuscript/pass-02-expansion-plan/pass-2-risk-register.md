# Pass 2 Risk Register

**Sprint:** SB-006A  
**Book:** Book 01 — Silver Bullet  
**Status:** Active expansion plan

## Purpose
Track risks that could emerge during Pass 2 expansion.

## Risk Register

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fictionalization Drift | High | Expanded specificity could move scenes too close to identifiable people/events. | Complete SB-010 before prose expansion. Use fictional names, places, institutions, and altered chronologies. |
| Trauma Overload | High | Expansion could intensify suffering without adding story value. | Use SB-003 dignity rules. Every dark scene must change plot, character, or continuity. |
| Institutional Cartooning | Medium | Process figures could become simplistic villains. | Preserve plausible incentives, careful language, and procedural neutrality. |
| Repetition | Medium | Social caution scenes may repeat the same beat. | Differentiate each loss by relationship, consequence, and evidence value. |
| Exposition Creep | Medium | Lantern/Living Anchor concepts could become over-explained. | Keep mythology in witness behavior and record/context motifs. |
| Ending Over-Resolution | High | Pass 2 could make Chapter 30 too clean. | Preserve partial restoration and Book 2 handoff. |
| Jack Passivity | High | More pressure scenes could make Jack feel acted upon only. | Add active choices: preserve, compare, refuse, ground, decide, trust selectively. |
| Evelyn Thinness | Medium | Evelyn may remain too abstract. | Add embodied fictional specificity while preserving composite safety. |
| Mara Convenience | Medium | Mara could feel like a late rescue device. | Seed her earlier through Devin/support-network context. |
| Record Fatigue | Medium | Too many documents could slow pacing. | Use documents only when they change scene stakes or context. |
