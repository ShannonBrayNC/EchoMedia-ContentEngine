# SB-006A Expansion Strategy

**Sprint:** SB-006A  
**Book:** Book 01 — Silver Bullet  
**Status:** Active expansion plan

## Purpose
Define the strategy for expanding SB-006 from a concise structural draft into a fuller Pass 2 manuscript.

## Expansion Principle
Pass 2 should add depth, not drift.

Every expansion must strengthen at least one of these functions:

- plot clarity
- character specificity
- trauma dignity
- fictionalization safety
- sensory immersion
- evidence/context continuity
- Widow Circle atmospheric pressure
- Book 2 handoff

## Target Result
Pass 2 should transform the current structural draft into a manuscript that feels more novelistic, embodied, and publication-track ready while remaining internal-development-only until SB-010 fictionalization controls are complete.

## Expansion Priorities

## 1. Add Scene Texture
SB-006 chapters are intentionally concise. Pass 2 should add:

- physical setting
- body continuity
- scene beats before and after key dialogue
- transitions that preserve emotional cause/effect
- small human actions that carry meaning

## 2. Deepen Supporting Characters
Pass 2 must give supporting characters more individuality before they perform major story functions.

Priority characters:

- Marcus
- Kendra
- Lena
- Devin
- Harlan
- Mara Venn
- Evelyn Blackwood

## 3. Strengthen Evelyn's Embodied Presence
Book 1 currently experiences Evelyn mostly through message and pressure. Pass 2 should add at least one embodied or strongly traceable scene that makes her fictional presence concrete without making her a one-to-one real-world analogue.

## 4. Make the Widow Circle More Atmospheric
The Circle should remain fictional and non-instructional. Pass 2 should deepen atmosphere through:

- repeated language
- social gestures
- symbolic concern
- silence
- careful distancing
- belief-driven interpretation

## 5. Preserve Institutional Plausibility
Institutions should not become cartoon villains. Pass 2 should show systems protecting themselves, reducing ambiguity, and acting on risk language in plausible ways.

## 6. Expand Body Continuity
Jack's pain, exhaustion, sleep disruption, food neglect, and physical limitation should carry across chapters without becoming repetitive shorthand.

## 7. Protect the Ending
Pass 2 must not turn Chapter 30 into a clean vindication. The ending should remain partial, costly, and trilogy-forward.

## Expansion Sequence

1. Complete SB-010 Fictionalization Pass.
2. Apply name, setting, institution, and specificity controls.
3. Expand Act I for character baseline and loss.
4. Expand Act II for relationship fracture and social echo.
5. Expand Act III for process pressure and body-cost scenes.
6. Expand Act IV for identity fracture with restraint.
7. Expand Act V for witness payoff and Book 2 handoff.
8. Run Producer Review refresh.

## Pass 2 Guardrails

- No public-facing excerpts before fictionalization pass.
- No direct real-world event mapping.
- No clean public vindication.
- No technology-as-savior ending.
- No trauma spectacle.
- No operational treatment of the Widow Circle.

## Producer Decision
Expansion strategy: **approved for controlled internal development**.
