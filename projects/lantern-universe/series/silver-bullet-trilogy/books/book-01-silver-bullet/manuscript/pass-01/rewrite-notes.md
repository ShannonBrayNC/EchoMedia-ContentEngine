# SB-006 Rewrite Notes

**Sprint:** SB-006  
**Book:** Book 01 — Silver Bullet  
**Pass:** Manuscript Rewrite Pass 1

## Purpose
Document the editorial intent, known limitations, and follow-up needs for the first structured manuscript draft.

## Drafting Result
Pass 1 created a complete 30-chapter structural prose draft aligned to SB-004 and SB-005.

## Primary Creative Outcome
The book now reads as a grounded psychological thriller/horror arc centered on Jack Mercer's loss of context, social displacement, institutional pressure, and movement toward witness preservation.

## Act Execution

### Act I — The Shot
Chapters 1-6 establish normalcy, the first wrong cue, Evelyn's carefully framed message, context loss, repeated concern language, and the first hard contradiction.

### Act II — The Echo
Chapters 7-12 show social temperature drop, story mutation, ally loss, harmful concern language, context-free evidence, and the echo becoming ambient.

### Act III — The Machine
Chapters 13-19 shift the story into formal notices, risk language, documentation burden, body-cost misreading, institutional flattening, partial records, and system-pattern recognition.

### Act IV — The Fracture
Chapters 20-25 focus on identity displacement, old wounds under new language, the quiet doubter, place-safety collapse, false mercy, and the low-point detail that survives.

### Act V — The Witness
Chapters 26-30 convert surviving evidence into a witness path, sequence restoration, human confirmation, counter-record creation, and Book 2 pattern expansion.

## Strengths of Pass 1

- Strong five-act structural fidelity.
- Jack's technical competence is integrated into witness behavior.
- Trauma is plot-active without reducing Jack to trauma alone.
- Evidence/context thread remains continuous.
- Book 2 doorway is clear.
- Living Anchor themes are foreshadowed but not over-explained.

## Known Limitations

- Pass 1 is intentionally concise; chapters need expansion in later prose passes.
- Supporting characters need deeper individuality in Pass 2.
- Evelyn is present primarily through message/pressure and should receive more embodied scene work in a later pass.
- Widow Circle atmosphere is functional but needs richer texture after Producer Review.
- Institutional scenes are structurally effective but should be varied in later drafts to avoid procedural sameness.
- Sensory continuity should be strengthened in Pass 2.

## Pass 2 Recommendations

1. Expand each chapter into full prose length.
2. Add more embodied scenes with Evelyn or her immediate social orbit.
3. Deepen Marcus, Kendra, Lena, Devin, Harlan, and Mara.
4. Add recurring motifs: refrigerator/vent/house sounds, paper, timestamps, empty rooms, careful language.
5. Increase physical environment specificity.
6. Preserve restraint; do not turn the ending into clean vindication.
7. Add line-level literary polish after Producer Review.

## Producer Review Needs

SB-007 should review:

- fictionalization and composite safety
- trauma dignity
- institutional parallels
- public reputation themes
- character continuity
- canon alignment
- Book 2 handoff

## Result
SB-006 establishes a complete draft foundation for Silver Bullet Book 1 and is ready for Producer Review planning.
