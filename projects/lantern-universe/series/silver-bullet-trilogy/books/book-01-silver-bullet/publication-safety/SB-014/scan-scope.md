# SB-014 Scan Scope

**Sprint:** SB-014  
**Book:** Book 01 — Silver Bullet  
**Status:** Scope complete

## Purpose
Define the safety scan boundary for Book 1 before any public-facing use.

## Material in Scope

- SB-011 Pass 2 manuscript folder
- SB-010 fictionalization controls
- SB-012 Producer Review Refresh
- SB-013 Evelyn composite controls
- Book 1 setting, character, artifact, and chronology labels

## Material Out of Scope

- Final line edit
- Copyedit
- Cover copy
- Sales description
- Query letter
- Audiobook sample
- Public excerpt selection
- Final legal review

These items require later gates.

## Scan Categories

1. Name, entity, and place scan
2. Artifact and correspondence-language scan
3. Chronology-distance scan
4. Public-positioning risk scan
5. Safety findings and remediation plan

## Review Standard
The manuscript should read as original fiction with composite characters, invented settings, invented organizations, fictional artifacts, and transformed chronology.

## Current Gate Principle
Passing SB-014 does not mean public use is automatically approved. SB-014 identifies what must be remediated before SB-016 final producer sign-off.

## Producer Decision
Scope: **approved**.
