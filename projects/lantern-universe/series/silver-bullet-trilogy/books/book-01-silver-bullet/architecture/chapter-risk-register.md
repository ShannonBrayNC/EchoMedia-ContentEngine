# Silver Bullet Book 1 Chapter Risk Register

**Sprint:** SB-004  
**Status:** Active risk register  
**Book:** Book 01 — Silver Bullet

## Purpose
Track chapter-level risks before prose drafting begins. This protects the book from canon drift, tone drift, repetition, and unsafe fictionalization drift.

## Risk Categories

| Risk | Meaning | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Canon Drift | Chapter contradicts Canon Bible, Character Registry, or Timeline Registry. | Validate against PUB-006, PUB-007, PUB-008. |
| Tone Drift | Chapter becomes too soft, too procedural, or too melodramatic. | Compare to SB-001 rewrite blueprint. |
| Trauma Spectacle | Chapter hurts Jack without deepening story or dignity. | Use SB-003 scene checklist. |
| Antagonist Overreach | Widow Circle material becomes too operational or cartoonish. | Use SB-002 fiction-only framing. |
| Exposition Dump | Chapter explains Lantern/mythology too directly. | Replace with witness, context, and record pressure. |
| Repetition | Chapter repeats a pressure beat without escalation. | Check act movement and scene type. |
| Clean Victory Too Soon | Chapter resolves too much before Book 1 ending. | Preserve trilogy handoff. |
| Legal/Likeness Sensitivity | Chapter feels too close to real people/events. | Producer Review and fictionalization review. |

## Chapter-Level Watchlist

| Chapter Range | Primary Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| 1-6 | Opening may be too slow or too explanatory. | Keep dread in small cues; establish Jack through action. |
| 7-12 | Social echo can become repetitive. | Make each loss different: ally, tone, evidence, public version. |
| 13-19 | Institutional scenes can feel dry. | Tie every process beat to human cost and record horror. |
| 20-25 | Fracture can become pure despair. | Preserve plot motion and one surviving detail. |
| 26-30 | Ending can become too clean. | Resolve witness survival, not full public vindication. |

## Producer Review Flags
Every chapter should be reviewed for:

- fictionalized composite risk
- trauma dignity
- character continuity
- evidence/witness continuity
- canon alignment
- trilogy handoff

## Drafting Gate
Before SB-005 scene cards begin, each chapter must have:

- act placement
- plot function
- trauma pressure
- antagonist pressure
- narrative distortion
- evidence/witness thread
- continuity impact
- Producer Review flag

## Result
The chapter architecture can proceed to SB-005 Scene Cards when this register shows no blocking risks.
