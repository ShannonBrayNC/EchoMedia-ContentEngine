## Place Safety Rules

1. Use fictional places consistently.
2. Do not use exact real addresses.
3. Do not use real county, courthouse, agency, or workplace names.
4. Do not anchor the story to a searchable real route.
5. Use place-state changes as fiction craft, not documentary mapping.
