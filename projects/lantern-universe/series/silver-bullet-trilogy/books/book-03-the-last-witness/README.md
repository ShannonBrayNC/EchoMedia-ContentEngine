# Book 03 — The Last Witness

**Sprint:** SB-009  
**Status:** Concept locked  
**Series:** Silver Bullet Trilogy

## Locked Title
**The Last Witness**

## Book Function
Book 3 resolves the Silver Bullet Trilogy by forcing Jack Mercer to decide what truth costs when records can be bent, witnesses can be broken, and the world prefers the story that requires the least courage.

## Trilogy Position

| Book | Function |
|---|---|
| Book 1 — Silver Bullet | Personal collapse and witness awakening. |
| Book 2 — The Echo Ledger | Pattern discovery and witness network formation. |
| Book 3 — The Last Witness | Final confrontation over truth survival, witness cost, and identity restoration without clean erasure of wounds. |

## Core Question
Can one wounded human witness preserve truth long enough for others to carry it, or will the final record become another weapon against the person who survived it?
