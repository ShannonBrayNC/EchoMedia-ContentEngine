# Widow Circle

**Sprint:** PUB-006  
**Status:** Canon-draft organization  
**Canon Area:** Organizations

## Core Definition
The Widow Circle is a malicious belief network that weaponizes gossip, accusation, social proof, ritualized moral certainty, and institutional fear.

## Locked Direction
The Widow Circle is not merely a group of criminals. Its members believe they are protecting society. That belief makes them more dangerous because cruelty becomes moral duty.

## Function
The Widow Circle targets people by turning social suspicion into operational reality. It spreads stories that make punishment appear inevitable before facts are established.

## Tools
The Widow Circle uses:

- whispered allegations
- selective evidence
- curated screenshots
- social pressure
- institutional reporting systems
- reputation poisoning
- isolation tactics
- ritualized language
- moral inversion

## Relationship to Silver Bullet
In Silver Bullet, the Widow Circle becomes the cult-like force that amplifies the Silver Bullet attack model. It does not merely accuse; it builds a world where the accusation feels pre-validated.

## Relationship to Narrative Corruption
The Widow Circle is a human engine of narrative corruption. It does not need supernatural power to create horror. It uses belief, fear, and systems.

## Producer Review Trigger
Any story that softens the Widow Circle into simple gossip, or makes it cartoonishly evil without ideology, requires review. The horror depends on ordinary people doing harm while believing they are righteous.
