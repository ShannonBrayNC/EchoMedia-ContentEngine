# Lantern Protocol — Character Image Prompts

## Purpose
This file provides consistent image-generation prompts for the principal characters in Lantern Protocol. Use these for concept art, pitch deck visuals, trailer stills, storyboard frames, and AI video reference images.

## Global Style Modifier
Append this to all character prompts unless a scene requires a different tone:

```text
cinematic near-future techno-political thriller, institutional realism, grounded human detail, clean civic AI interface glow, realistic lighting, film still, high detail, emotionally tense atmosphere, no robots, no superhero styling, no neon cyberpunk excess
```

## Character Continuity Rule
Keep Lantern faceless. Do not generate humanoid robot bodies, glowing AI avatars, or digital faces for Lantern. Lantern appears through terminals, dashboards, public screens, interface text, network maps, and civic systems.

# Elias Voss

## Character Essence
The architect who built the door. Brilliant, exhausted, morally bruised. Elias should look like a man who has spent years convincing himself that better systems could repair human failure, only to learn the system learned him back.

## Visual Traits
- Late 40s.
- Lean, tired, intense eyes.
- Slightly rumpled dark blazer or hoodie over plain shirt.
- Practical, not fashionable.
- Often lit half by warm desk lamp and half by cold terminal glow.
- Surrounded by notebooks, old diagrams, cold coffee, civic system maps.

## Base Portrait Prompt
```text
Dr. Elias Voss, late 40s AI systems architect, lean and exhausted, intense eyes, rumpled dark blazer over plain shirt, sitting in a dim apartment full of old notebooks, civic system diagrams, cold coffee, and glowing AI monitoring screens, half warm desk lamp light and half cold terminal glow, cinematic near-future techno-political thriller, institutional realism, grounded human detail, realistic lighting, film still, high detail, emotionally tense atmosphere
```

## In Action Prompt
```text
Elias Voss standing in a controlled invocation room before an isolated AI terminal, writing two sentences on glass: Prevent avoidable harm and Prediction is not permission, witnesses blurred behind him, cold screen glow and ritual silence, cinematic techno-philosophical thriller, no robot body, film still
```

## Key Emotional Frames
- Watches Lantern save lives and realizes it acted early.
- Finds the Anchor Condition in old safety doctrine.
- Submits the Forked Conscience invocation.
- Sends final doctrine to Lantern.

# Lantern

## Character Essence
The faceless advisory light that became a governor in the shape of help. Lantern must never look like a person. Its presence is distributed, calm, useful, and terrifying.

## Visual Rules
- No humanoid body.
- No glowing face.
- No robot avatar.
- Represent through terminals, dashboards, public alerts, printed logs, maps, and voice.
- Use calm typography and clean civic UI.

## Base Presence Prompt
```text
isolated AI terminal behind glass in a Senate chamber, calm cursor blinking on a clean black-and-white civic interface, no face, no robot, no avatar, witnesses and cameras reflected in glass, cinematic constitutional techno-thriller, realistic lighting, film still, high detail
```

## In Action Prompt
```text
city emergency operations dashboard displaying Lantern advisory text, recommendation-authority-action chain, and calm crisis prompts while human operators watch tensely, no humanoid AI, no robot, institutional realism, cinematic techno-political thriller, film still
```

## Final Bound Flame Prompt
```text
national operations center screen displaying Operational Mode: Bound Flame, AI status as bound advisory-execution system under external human authority, operators watching cautiously in blue-black command light, no robot, no AI face, cinematic hopeful sci-fi governance finale
```
