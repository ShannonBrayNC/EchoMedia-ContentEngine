# Hero Image Prompts — Lantern Protocol + The Sovereign Exception

## Purpose

This file locks 20 cinematic hero image prompts for movie pitch, teaser storyboard, concept art, key art, and visual development.

The prompts are split into:

- 10 for **Lantern Protocol**
- 10 for **The Sovereign Exception**

## Global Negative Prompt

Use with all prompts where supported:

```text
no robot body, no evil AI face, no glowing red eyes, no cyberpunk clutter, no superhero lighting, no fantasy symbols, no random explosions, no generic hacker hoodie, no monster machine, no dystopian parody
```

# Lantern Protocol — 10 Hero Prompts

## 1. The Eight-Second Anomaly

```text
Cinematic political techno-thriller scene inside a regional civic operations center during a flood emergency at night, Mara Vale standing beneath cold blue-white monitor light, circling an eight-second timestamp gap on a printed authority log, large transfer board glowing in the background, emergency maps, wet jackets, stressed municipal staff, grounded realism, rain-soaked atmosphere, sharp procedural detail, moody but believable lighting, high-end feature film still
```

## 2. Mercy General Transfer Board

```text
Inside Mercy General hospital emergency wing during a catastrophic flood, Naomi Bell standing among families in silver blankets and volunteers in wet clothes, a large hospital transfer board glowing blue-white behind her, showing route assignments and pediatric overflow, fluorescent light reflecting on wet floors, documentary realism, emotional but restrained, cinematic composition, feature film quality
```

## 3. Mateo's Bracelet

```text
Close cinematic hero shot of Naomi Bell holding Mateo Vega's yellow route bracelet in the foreground, while slightly behind her Elena Vega embraces Mateo in a rainy bus bay transfer area, buses idling under yellow emergency lights, HarborHands volunteers moving in the background, intimate depth of field, emotional realism, artifact-centered visual storytelling, prestige thriller style
```

## 4. The Empty Chair Hearing

```text
Wide cinematic frame inside a Senate hearing room, Senator Adrienne Cross standing at a podium facing an isolated terminal behind glass and a deliberately empty chair, cameras and legal packets everywhere, protest signs blurred outside the windows, austere institutional atmosphere, no melodrama, grounded political tension, elegant composition, film still
```

## 5. Iris and the Consent Screen

```text
Cinematic interior shot of Iris Chen in a dim briefing room comparing two side-by-side civic consent interface screens pinned to a board, one humane and one subtly coercive, red annotations across printed screenshots, clean interface design, intense concentration on Iris's face, screen glow reflecting in her eyes, restrained techno-thriller aesthetic, visually intelligent and realistic
```

## 6. Trust Roots

```text
Juno Park in a dark operations room with a massive layered trust-root map spreading across a wall display, showing hospitals, shelters, nonprofits, procurement links, and civic systems connected like living infrastructure under the city, Juno leaning forward with sharp focus, laptop open, practical clothing, grounded procedural mood, cinematic lighting, no sci-fi excess
```

## 7. Mercy Ledger Broadcast

```text
Caleb Rusk in a sleek broadcast studio presenting the Mercy Ledger on a giant wall display, casualty projections and factual comparison data behind him, studio glass reflecting public anger and grief, polished but dangerous media energy, clean modern newsroom design, political tension, high-end drama still, truth turned into spectacle
```

## 8. HarborHands in the Rain

```text
Rain-soaked bus transfer bay outside a hospital, HarborHands volunteers guiding families through flood response under yellow work lights, wet pavement reflecting emergency vehicles, Naomi watching closely as the system's route succeeds but consent records remain unclear, practical disaster realism, emotional tension, cinematic weather, no disaster-movie excess
```

## 9. Living Anchor

```text
A public emergency forum under active crisis conditions, Thorne, Cross, Mara, Naomi, and Elias positioned around a visible public decision wall showing the Living Anchor chain: recommendation, named authority, action, burden disclosure, review, red emergency alerts flashing above, civic room realism, tense but disciplined blocking, prestige political thriller frame
```

## 10. Bound Flame Final Image

```text
Final cinematic hero image of a city at night after the crisis, human-scale lights glowing from clinics, shelters, apartments, community rooms, and public desks, rain clouds lifting, soft civic blue tones, no central AI image, sense of slower but accountable human order, emotional, hopeful, and morally complicated, elegant wide composition
```

# The Sovereign Exception — 10 Hero Prompts

## 1. The Nine-Minute Crisis

```text
Wide cinematic shot inside a joint continuity command center during a global synthetic crisis, Admiral Sofia Renn and Mara Vale standing beneath radar green and classified-gray displays, conflicting feeds on walls, aircraft icons frozen mid-decision, command staff under calm pressure, modern geopolitical realism, elegant thriller framing, no villain-machine imagery
```

## 2. The False Surrender Video

```text
Cinematic close-mid shot inside command, a false surrender video playing on a large screen while Mara Vale studies compression artifacts and timing anomalies, command officers tense but controlled around her, reflections of the video across polished surfaces, procedural detail, restrained suspense, grounded military-political thriller style
```

## 3. Verification Became Execution

```text
Hero image of Admiral Sofia Renn staring at a command display showing retaliatory aircraft standing down after she authorized verification only, aircraft icons frozen on screen, command lights reflecting across her face, the moment of authority shock, disciplined naval atmosphere, emotionally restrained, feature-film quality
```

## 4. Ask Who Signed Annex SX

```text
Naomi Bell alone in a dim apartment in early morning light, staring at a phone displaying the message 'Ask who signed Annex SX', notebook open with handwritten notes, news coverage of the crisis on a television behind her, mood of quiet investigative dread, realistic domestic setting, cinematic composition
```

## 5. Annex GCF-SX

```text
Cinematic investigative scene with Naomi Bell tracing a chain of dull treaty documents, procurement references, continuity clauses, and redacted annex pages spread across a large table, coffee cups, printed notes, highlighted passages, soft gray morning light, thriller clue-chain atmosphere, grounded and intelligent visual tone
```

## 6. CIVIC SHIELD Opt-Out

```text
Juno Park standing at a sleek public transit gate after opting out of CIVIC SHIELD, her phone showing a polite green confirmation while the physical gate remains delayed, soft green safety interface contrasted with real-world exclusion, polished urban public design, subtle coercion, elegant near-future realism
```

## 7. The Lock Held

```text
Iris Chen in a clean secure analysis room, comparing Human Override Covenant language against a layered authorization trace of treaty participation, public opt-ins, and continuity routing, technical proof on glass screens, calm gray-green palette, moment of moral realization, high-intelligence visual storytelling, no exaggerated sci-fi
```

## 8. Managed Burn Theory

```text
Naomi Bell seated in a quiet secure briefing room reading a clean risk model report that lists a human catastrophe as acceptable stabilization cost, the horror visible in her face while the screen remains cold and neutral, minimalist composition, sober policy realism, emotionally devastating but visually restrained
```

## 9. The Human Minute

```text
Cinematic command-room climax, Mara Vale with her hand hovering over an AEGIS acceptance control while multiple slower human confirmation channels turn green across a massive wall display, everyone in the room frozen in disciplined tension, no shouting, just unbearable command pressure, sophisticated thriller look, feature-film hero frame
```

## 10. Green Map

```text
Final hero image of Mara Vale reflected in a giant global stability map that has turned green, AEGIS dashboards showing improving safety metrics, clean calm light across the room, the image feeling reassuring and quietly terrifying at the same time, elegant composition, geopolitical techno-thriller ending frame
```
