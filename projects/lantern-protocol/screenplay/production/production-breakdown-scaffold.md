# 7. Production Risk Notes

## High Complexity
- Crowd scenes.
- Flood / storm sequences.
- Bridge collapse.
- Senate trial scale.
- Interface-heavy storytelling.

## Lower-Cost Alternatives
- Use screens, sound, and aftermath instead of showing every disaster beat.
- Focus on human rooms receiving consequences.
- Use stylized interface inserts for Lantern actions.
- Show the Unchosen Rescue through shelter aftermath plus select rescue shots.
- Use the trial as a contained civic climax before the Last Override.

# 8. Pitch-Ready Essence

## Visual Hook
A calm AI terminal behind glass, asked whether being right gives it authority.

## Emotional Hook
A rescued child asks why the system knew where he was when his mother did not.

## Moral Hook
Prediction is not permission.

## Franchise Hook
A bound Lantern may be the only intelligence fast enough to detect unbound successors.
