# Chapter 011
## The Fourteen-Minute Window

### Narrative Purpose
Transform the bug-tracker leak into the first true investigation of The Null.

The chapter proves that information escaped before anyone understood its value.

---

## Opening Image

A screen recording replaying fourteen minutes of public exposure.

Not dramatic.

Just a boring administrative interface.

The horror comes from realizing who viewed it.

---

## Scene 1
### Reconstruction

Jack rebuilds the exposure timeline.

Christina identifies:

- Legislative network access
- Contractor VPN access
- Archive mirror access

Nobody knows which matters.

---

## Scene 2
### Contamination Debate

Maggie insists all data be labeled.

Vanessa argues contamination concerns are slowing them down.

Conflict escalates.

---

## Scene 3
### Missing Attachment

The deleted attachment is discovered.

Not the file itself.

Evidence it existed.

---

## Scene 4
### Access Shadow

One anonymous viewer downloaded something.

The current system shows no downloadable content.

---

## Ending Hook

The missing attachment has a file name:

CELIA_AUTH_HISTORY.xlsx
