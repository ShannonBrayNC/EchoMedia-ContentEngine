# Book 1 Free Funnel Strategy

## Purpose

Book 1 should create audience trust and bring readers into the Lantern Protocol universe with the least possible friction.

## Funnel path

1. Reader/listener discovers Book 1.
2. Book 1 is free or extremely low-cost.
3. Reader reaches the end and understands the stakes of Lantern.
4. End matter points to Book 2, the site, and the broader Lantern Protocol ecosystem.
5. Reader converts into Book 2, Book 3, newsletter, site, or future launch audience.

## Book 1 must include

- A strong opening hook.
- Clean prose formatting.
- No internal artifacts.
- Clear series identity.
- A reason to continue.
- Book 2 preview or teaser.
- Call to action.

## Suggested calls to action

- Continue with Book 2.
- Visit the Lantern Protocol / EchoMedia.ai site.
- Join the update list.
- Listen to related audio/video material.
- Follow future releases.

## Listening review use

The ElevenReader pass is both QA and market rehearsal. During listening, track:

- Where attention drops.
- Where names or concepts blur together.
- Where AI/system language becomes too abstract.
- Where character voices are not distinct enough.
- Where the story sounds like notes instead of finished prose.

## Conversion notes

The Book 1 ending should not feel like an abrupt advertisement. It should feel like a door opening into a larger room.

Recommended final movement:

1. Finish the emotional/plot beat.
2. Provide a short author note.
3. Give a concise Book 2 teaser.
4. Offer the reader a next step.
