# Lantern Protocol Publishing Pipeline

This folder tracks the production workflow for turning the Lantern Protocol trilogy into listener-ready, reader-ready, and market-ready releases.

## Publishing principles

1. **Separate audio review from retail publishing.**
   - ElevenReader files should be clean narration manuscripts.
   - KDP files should be retail manuscripts with front matter, back matter, metadata, and launch assets.

2. **Use Book 1 as the discovery funnel.**
   - Make Book 1 as free or low-friction as possible.
   - Use audio listening to catch continuity, pacing, missing transitions, and awkward prose.
   - Convert readers/listeners into Books 2 and 3 through a clear call to action.

3. **Price Books 2 and 3 as premium follow-through.**
   - Book 1 earns trust.
   - Books 2 and 3 monetize the audience that wants the rest of the system.

4. **Keep screenplay artifacts separate.**
   - Screenplay exports support trailers, pitch materials, faceless video, cinematic treatment, and future adaptation packages.
   - Do not upload raw screenplay format as the primary ElevenReader audiobook manuscript unless the goal is explicitly a screenplay reading.

5. **Preserve future publisher optionality.**
   - Self-publish the first Lantern Protocol trilogy deliberately.
   - Keep the future second series positioned for professional publishing and stronger external marketing.
