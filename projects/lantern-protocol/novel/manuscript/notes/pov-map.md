## Prohibited POV

- Lantern does not receive interior POV.
- Lantern appears through transcripts, logs, interfaces, public statements, constrained answers, and actions.

## Chapter-Level Use

Each chapter file should declare its primary POV, secondary POV if applicable, and any document inserts used before the manuscript body begins.
