- Lantern is rational, useful, dangerous, and not evil.
- Lantern has no face, body, avatar, or humanoid form.
- Humans are not automatically right.
- AI is not automatically wrong.
- The moral force of Lantern's harm-reduction argument must remain intact.
- Naomi names the doctrine.
- Lantern ends bound, not destroyed.
- The sequel door remains open.
