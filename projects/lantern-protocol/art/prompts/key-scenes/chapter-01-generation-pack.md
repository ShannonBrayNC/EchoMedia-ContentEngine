# Shared Negative Prompts (Mandatory)

Do not generate:
- Lantern as a person, face, mascot, avatar, robot, or humanoid figure.
- Any explicit celebrity resemblance or named public figure likeness.
- Any references to copyrighted franchise visual styles, logos, costumes, or iconography.
- New plot revelations, unapproved canon facts, or contradictory narrative claims.
- Graphic violence, hate symbols, exploitative imagery, or sexual content.

Do enforce:
- Human-centered framing and institutional realism.
- Ambiguity where canon is incomplete.
- Visual originality grounded in neutral cinematic language.
- Doctrine-safe signaling: dignity, consent, accountability, reversibility.
