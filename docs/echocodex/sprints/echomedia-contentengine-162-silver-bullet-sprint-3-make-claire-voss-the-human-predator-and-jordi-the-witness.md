## Required Story Beats

### Claire Voss Villain Layer

Claire must operate through three masks:

1. Public Claire: scared, calm, safety-language fluent.
2. Private Claire: coordinates calls, edits evidence, stalks Jordi, seeds reports.
3. Dark Claire: understands the system only needs a trigger.

Required villain doctrine:

> The law does not need to believe me forever. It only needs to believe me first.

### Jordi Targeting

Jordi is stalked or monitored, then reframed as part of Jack's pattern.

Required scene:
- Jordi calls from a bathroom, car, gas station, or workplace.
- Claire is physically nearby or has clearly been following/contacting her.
- Jordi is afraid to help because helping Jack makes her look recruited.

Required line:

> I am not evidence. I am your friend. But I saw what I saw.

### Manufactured Evidence

- edited audio
- staged text
- alarm-company call log

- screenshot packet
- spoofed or misleading phone contact
- third-party `concerned witness` statement
