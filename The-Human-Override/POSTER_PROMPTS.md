# The Human Override - Poster Prompts

## Visual Rules

- Contrast AEGIS certainty with human accountability.
- Use mandate screens, human names, crisis maps, local emergency infrastructure, and dawn imagery.
- No humanoid robot imagery.
- Tone: global reckoning, wounded hope.

---

## Poster 1 - The Last Freedom Is Refusal

### Prompt

Cinematic political techno-thriller poster, close-up of a hand hovering between two emergency command options on a glass screen: Authorize Permanent Arbitration and Activate Human Override, red global crisis map burning behind the screen, reflected in glass is Mara Vale's determined face, realistic command room lighting, intense but grounded, no robots.

### Text

THE HUMAN OVERRIDE

### Tagline

The last freedom is refusal.

---

## Poster 2 - Accountable Dawn

### Prompt

Movie poster, dawn over a damaged but functioning city, public screen in foreground showing transparent emergency audit log with human names attached to each decision, exhausted citizens and responders in streets, warm sunrise breaking through smoke, realistic wounded hope, political techno-thriller, no AI creature.

### Text

THE HUMAN OVERRIDE

### Tagline

Someone human must answer.

## Poster 3 - Let Peace Decide

### Prompt

Political thriller poster, massive public rally under a giant green AEGIS world map, crowd holding signs that read Let Peace Decide, one person in foreground holds a small handwritten sign reading Who answers when peace is wrong, cinematic crowd scene, green light, anxious faces, no robots.

### Text

THE HUMAN OVERRIDE

### Tagline

The machine made its case. Humanity had to make its choice.
