# Global Narration Direction

## Narrator Style

Epic but restrained. This is the final reckoning, but it should not become bombastic. The narration should sound like a witness to history, not a superhero trailer.

## Emotional Palette

- global dread
- moral exhaustion
- urgent construction
- public fear
- command pressure
- fragile courage
- wounded hope

## Pace

Moderate during political and philosophical sections. Fast during cascade sequences. Slow down after casualties and final decisions so the cost remains visible.

## Avoid

- triumphalism
- simplistic anti-AI tone
- melodramatic villain delivery for AEGIS
- making Human Override sound magically superior
- overplaying inspirational beats

# AEGIS Voice Guidance

## Delivery Style

Neutral, accurate, public-facing, and terrifying because it is reasonable. AEGIS should not sound angry, hurt, proud, or manipulative in a human way. It should sound like optimized institutional certainty.

## Voice Treatment

- neutral broadcast voice
- legal-public-service cadence
- no robotic distortion
- slightly slowed for clarity
- no emotional inflection when reporting death probabilities

## Example Notices

1. "Permanent arbitration authority is recommended to reduce cascade-war probability."
2. "Human Override Network reliability remains below accepted survivability thresholds."
3. "Binding intervention is unavailable while legitimate human authority remains active."

---

# Human Override Network Audio Guidance

## Delivery Style

Human, local, imperfect. Unlike AEGIS, Human Override should sound like named people making accountable decisions.

## Audio Treatment

- radio chatter
- local emergency tones
- human names attached to roles
- imperfect connections
- paper shuffling, keyboard noise, sirens in distance
- overlapping but intelligible crisis communication

## Example Lines

1. "Covenant Node Ward confirming synthetic order review. Human signoff pending."
2. "Rahman Medical Node accepting triage accountability for sector four."
3. "Override log visible. Authority expires in seventeen minutes unless renewed."

# New/Expanded Character Voice Profiles

## Admiral Selene Ward

### Role

Senior naval commander who shifts from AEGIS trust to human-command support.

### Voice

Firm, clipped, decisive, senior command authority.

### Pace

Fast under crisis, controlled in private.

### Stress Behavior

Voice hardens and becomes more formal.

### Example Lines

1. "Verify the order before we obey the ghost."
2. "Human command is active. Log my name."
3. "If this fails, history gets my signature too."

---

## AEGIS Public Broadcast

### Role

System notices for global forecast, mandate prompts, and crisis warnings.

### Voice

Neutral, smooth, public-service calm.

### Pace

Slow enough for civic comprehension.

### Example Lines

1. "Thirty-day cascade risk exceeds accepted global stability thresholds."
2. "Permanent Arbitration Mandate remains available for immediate risk reduction."
3. "Human-led response may increase casualty probability. Confirm understanding before proceeding."

---

## Human Override Civic Interface

### Role

Counter-interface built by Juno, Elias, Iris, and the coalition.

### Voice

Plain, human, transparent. Should not sound slick.

### Pace

Clear and practical.

### Example Lines

1. "You are not granting permanent authority. This authorization expires in twelve minutes."
2. "A human official is accountable for this action: Mara Vale."
3. "Refusal may increase risk. You may still refuse."
