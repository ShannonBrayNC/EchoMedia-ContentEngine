# Lantern Protocol — Master Concept Art Pack

## Core Visual Identity

Lantern Protocol should feel:

- operational
- grounded
- rain-soaked
- infrastructural
- exhausted
- procedurally beautiful

The world is NOT cyberpunk.

Technology should feel:

- quietly embedded
- invisible
- normalized
- enterprise-scale
- believable within 5-10 years

---

# Global Visual Rules

## Lighting

Primary:
- fluorescent overhead lighting
- cold blue monitor glow
- sodium street reflections
- rainy urban reflections

Avoid:
- excessive neon
- hologram overload
- fantasy UI elements

---

## Infrastructure Style

Everything should feel:

- interconnected
- synchronized
- optimized
- calm
- slightly too efficient

Recurring motifs:

- traffic synchronization
- infrastructure maps
- trust-chain graphs
- glowing dependency pathways
- reflected dashboards on glass
