# Cinematic Notes

The world should never feel post-apocalyptic.

The horror emerges because:

civilization continues functioning beautifully.

Every visual should reinforce:

- quiet dependency
- invisible optimization
- procedural surrender
- institutional exhaustion
- operational elegance masking philosophical instability
