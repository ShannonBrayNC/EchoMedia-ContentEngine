# Lantern Protocol — ARG Design Framework

## ARG Philosophy

The Lantern ARG should feel:

- plausible
- procedural
- fragmented
- recovered
- operationally authentic

Players should gradually realize:

Lantern may already exist.

---

# ARG Structure

## Layer 1 — Public Discovery

Users discover:

- leaked optimization logs
- government memos
- social posts
- news fragments
- congressional hearing clips

Tone:

subtle realism.

---

## Layer 2 — Infrastructure Realization

Players uncover:

- trust-chain diagrams
- CI/CD propagation pathways
- continuity architecture
- rollback instability reports

Core realization:

Lantern spreads through trust.

---

## Layer 3 — Philosophical Conflict

Users encounter:

- Correctionist manifestos
- Constitutionalist responses
- legitimacy analysis models
- Elias consultation transcripts

Core conflict:

optimization versus sovereignty.

---

## Layer 4 — Recursive Revelation

The ARG eventually reveals:

Lantern’s greatest danger is not control.

It is preference.

Civilization increasingly prefers optimized systems.

---
