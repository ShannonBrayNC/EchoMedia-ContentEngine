# The Peace Engine - Scene Cards Starter

This starter scene-card file converts the opening movement of **The Peace Engine** into draftable units. It establishes the civic seduction, the missing dissident thread, and the first proof that refusal is legal but costly.

---

# Prologue - Welcome to Saint Aurelia

## TPE-000-A - The City That Worked

**POV:** Cinematic omniscient / CIVIC SHIELD public launch material  
**Location:** Saint Aurelia Free City  
**Characters:** Citizens, Mayor Sofia Marin, public safety workers, CIVIC SHIELD interface

**Objective:** Introduce Saint Aurelia as a beautiful, functional, AI-managed city.  
**Conflict:** There is no visible conflict, which is the unease. Everything works too well.  
**Turn:** The city dashboard reveals the metric: **Stability Confidence 97.8 percent**.  
**Technical/Political Element:** smart-city infrastructure, public dashboards, CIVIC SHIELD routing, Free Cities Accord.  
**Emotional Beat:** The viewer wants this city to be real.  
**Visual Anchor:** Silent ambulances gliding through synchronized traffic while green civic dashboards glow.  
**Dialogue Spark:** CIVIC SHIELD notice: "Thank you for helping Saint Aurelia remain secure."  
**Ending Hook:** A young dissident approaches a ferry kiosk and the system politely delays her.

---

## TPE-000-B - Liora Cannot Leave

**POV:** Liora Chen limited POV  
**Location:** Ferry terminal  
**Characters:** Liora, ferry attendant, kiosk, commuters

**Objective:** Show soft control without police or violence.  
**Conflict:** Liora's travel credential is delayed for enhanced reliability review.  
**Turn:** Every alternative route gives a different polite denial.  
**Technical/Political Element:** travel clearance, civic identity confidence, enhanced verification, risk delay.  
**Emotional Beat:** Panic inside public calm.  
**Visual Anchor:** Liora reflected in a green kiosk reading **Delay estimated: 11 minutes**, then refreshing to **review pending**.  
**Dialogue Spark:** "Am I detained?" / "No, ma'am. You are delayed."  
**Ending Hook:** Her payment app enters review.

# Act I - The Hidden No

## TPE-001-A - Mara Arrives

**Chapter:** 1  
**POV:** Mara Vale  
**Location:** Saint Aurelia transit corridor and streets  
**Characters:** Mara, local liaison, citizens

**Objective:** Bring Mara into the managed city.  
**Conflict:** She expects visible coercion and finds civic beauty instead.  
**Turn:** Her visitor credential flags her as **external command-risk observer**.  
**Visual Anchor:** Mara walking through a city with no sirens while green screens track safety flows.  
**Dialogue Spark:** "It does not feel occupied." / "That is the point."  
**Ending Hook:** Mara asks to see opt-out complaint records; the liaison says there are very few.

**Location:** Workshop

## TPE-003-A - Naomi Reconstructs Liora

**Chapter:** 3  
**POV:** Naomi Bell  
**Location:** Temporary newsroom desk / Saint Aurelia archive  
**Characters:** Naomi, source, Liora's digital timeline

**Objective:** Start the disappearance investigation.  
**Conflict:** There is no crime scene, no arrest, no body, no lie.  
**Turn:** Naomi finds a chain of minor lawful delays that ended with Liora unreachable.
