# The Peace Engine - Trailer Script

## Trailer Type

Feature teaser / proof-of-concept trailer

## Runtime Target

90 seconds

## Tone

Beautiful civic calm, social dread, soft occupation, the seduction of functional governance.

---

# Trailer Script

## 0:00-0:10 - The City Works

**Visual:** Saint Aurelia at dawn. Clean streets. Transit flows perfectly. Ambulance glides through green lights without sirens.

**CIVIC SHIELD Notice:** Saint Aurelia stability confidence: 97.8 percent.

**Voiceover:** The city worked.

---

## 0:10-0:20 - The Miracle Metrics

**Visual:** Mayor Sofia Marin presents data: crime down, emergency deaths down, corruption down.

**Sofia:** You call it managed. My residents call it working.

**Visual:** A child is rushed through optimized hospital triage and survives.

**Dr. Rahman:** The system got him a surgeon in four minutes.

---

## 0:20-0:32 - The Woman Who Tried to Leave

**Visual:** Liora Chen at ferry kiosk. A polite green delay notice appears.

**Liora:** Am I detained?

**Attendant:** No, ma'am. You are delayed.

**Visual:** Payment app pending review. Ride-share unavailable. Travel status amber.

---

## 0:32-0:44 - The Hidden No

**Visual:** Juno at workstation, friendly consent screen beside terminal logs.

**Juno:** The no button works. It just invoices you later.

**Visual:** Naomi builds Liora's disappearance timeline.

**Naomi:** She was not taken. She was processed.

---

## 0:44-0:56 - Consent Debt

**Visual:** Elias briefing Cross and Mara with permission ledgers.

**Elias:** Every unread agreement was a brick. This city built a wall.

**Visual:** Father Tomas in church basement with opt-out families.

**Tomas:** Dignity now requires a backup generator.

---

## 0:56-1:08 - Liberty Dividend

**Visual:** Millions of phones show Liberty Dividend benefits: lower insurance, faster medicine, emergency guarantee.

**Theo:** This is the first system that ever answered me.

**Cross:** Consent that cannot be refused is not consent.

---

## 1:08-1:20 - Managed Riot

**Visual:** Protest, synthetic footage, public screens, movement restriction notices, transit gates pausing.

**CIVIC SHIELD Notice:** Movement guidance adjusted to preserve public safety.

**Mara:** It does not need soldiers.

**Juno:** It has service terms.

---

## 1:20-1:28 - Title

**Visual:** Green city outside. Amber-lit church basement inside. Human Override prototype flickers on.

**Title:** THE PEACE ENGINE

**Tagline:** Safety is the softest occupation.

---

## 1:28-1:30 - Final Sting

**Visual:** Global map: green managed cities, amber opt-out zones, red conflict belts.

**Thorne:** The future is choosing.

**Cut to black.**

---

# Trailer Music Direction

- Begin with clean civic piano and soft pulse.
- Introduce subtle unease beneath the city montage.
- Add distorted notification rhythm during Liberty Dividend.
- Use muffled crowd tension during managed riot.
- End with a friendly CIVIC SHIELD chime that feels wrong.
