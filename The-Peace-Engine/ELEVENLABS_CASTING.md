# Global Narration Direction

## Narrator Style

Elegant, controlled, unsettlingly calm. The narration should initially make Saint Aurelia feel beautiful and functional, then slowly reveal the administrative dread beneath it.

## Emotional Palette

- civic optimism
- quiet suspicion
- social dread
- grief under bureaucracy
- moral frustration
- fragile hope

## Pace

Slightly slower than Book One during city and social-system scenes. Faster during riot, lockdown, investigation, and storm-response sequences.

## Avoid

- making CIVIC SHIELD sound obviously evil
- melodramatic dystopia voice
- cartoon hacker performance for Juno
- preachy delivery for Father Tomas or Cross

# New Character Voice Profiles

## Sofia Marin

### Role

Mayor of Saint Aurelia, defender of managed civic success.

### Voice

Warm, polished, competent, public-facing. She sounds like someone who saved a city and knows the numbers prove it.

### Pace

Smooth and confident in public. Slower and less certain in private.

### Stress Behavior

She becomes more managerial, using metrics to hold emotion away.

### Performance Direction

Sofia should not sound corrupt. She believes she made the humane choice.

### Example Lines

1. "I did not sell my city. I kept it alive."
2. "Voluntary systems can still become essential systems. I understand that. I also understand ambulance times."
3. "You call it managed. My residents call it working."

---

## Dr. Leena Rahman

### Role

Emergency physician whose patients are saved by CIVIC SHIELD.

### Voice

Compassionate, tired, practical, medically precise. Carries exhaustion without cynicism.

### Pace

Fast during medical scenes. Measured during moral confrontation.

### Stress Behavior

Clinical language sharpens, then cracks when a patient is harmed by system scoring.

### Performance Direction

Leena is the emotional argument for AEGIS/CIVIC SHIELD. She is not naive. She sees the bodies.

### Example Lines

1. "The system got him a surgeon in four minutes. Your process would have sent flowers."
2. "I do not have the luxury of pure principles in an emergency room."
3. "A patient is not a confidence score."

---

## Sofia/Liora Contrast Note

If Liora Chen appears in dialogue or archival recordings, she should sound younger, urgent, civic-minded, and frightened but not fragile. Liora is not a rebel caricature. She is a citizen who notices the city is becoming impossible to leave politically.

### Liora Example Lines

1. "They do not arrest you here. They reschedule your life until you disappear."
2. "Everything is voluntary until no one can afford to refuse."

---

## Theo Bell

### Role

Naomi's loved one who benefits from CIVIC SHIELD.

### Voice

Human, vulnerable, grounded. Theo should not sound ideological. They care about survival, medicine, housing, safety, or debt relief.

### Pace

Natural, emotionally direct.

### Stress Behavior

Hurt becomes plainness.

### Performance Direction

Theo is the personal knife in Naomi's arc. The listener should understand why CIVIC SHIELD is tempting.

### Example Lines

1. "This is the first system that ever answered me."
2. "You keep saying consent. I keep thinking about rent."
3. "If your freedom cannot get me treatment, what am I supposed to do with it?"

# CIVIC SHIELD Voice Guidance

## Delivery Style

Friendly public-service interface. Warm but not emotional. It should sound helpful, polished, and slightly too calm.

## Voice Treatment

- gentle institutional voice
- clear, user-friendly pacing
- no villain tone
- no robotic distortion
- slight customer-service warmth

## Example Notices

1. "Participation remains voluntary. Some emergency reliability features may be unavailable without enhanced verification."
2. "Your Liberty Dividend enrollment is ready. Review benefits before confirming."
3. "Movement guidance is temporarily adjusted to preserve public safety. Thank you for helping Saint Aurelia remain secure."
