# The Peace Engine - Poster Prompts

## Visual Rules

- The dystopia should look desirable.
- CIVIC SHIELD is represented by friendly interfaces, public dashboards, benefits screens, and civic infrastructure.
- No robots or overt monster imagery.
- Tone: beautiful civic calm with hidden coercion.

---

## Poster 1 - Safety Is the Softest Occupation

### Prompt

Cinematic political techno-thriller poster, beautiful clean smart city at sunrise, synchronized transit and calm citizens, foreground public kiosk displays Participation Voluntary, reflected in the kiosk screen a worried citizen's travel status reads Delayed Pending Reliability Review, warm dawn light mixed with green civic dashboard glow, realistic near-future city, no robots.

### Text

THE PEACE ENGINE

### Tagline

Safety is the softest occupation.

---

## Poster 2 - The Liberty Dividend

### Prompt

Close-up movie poster of a human thumb hovering over a smartphone accept button for CIVIC SHIELD Liberty Dividend, benefits listed: lower insurance, faster medical routing, travel priority, emergency guarantee, tiny decline option half-hidden beneath warning text, friendly bright UI, dark blurred room behind, ominous realistic social techno-thriller.

### Text

THE PEACE ENGINE

### Tagline

The cage has benefits.

---

## Poster 3 - The Woman Who Tried to Leave

### Prompt

Political thriller poster, young civic dissident standing alone at a clean ferry terminal kiosk, green delay notice glowing on screen, commuters passing calmly around her, no police, no violence, beautiful smart city visible through glass behind her, realistic cinematic dread, administrative control, no robots.

### Text

THE PEACE ENGINE

### Tagline

No one arrested her. The systems simply delayed her.

### Prompt

### Text

THE PEACE ENGINE

### Tagline

Freedom now requires a backup generator.

## Poster 5 - Green City, Amber People

### Prompt

Minimalist poster, global map of managed cities glowing green and opt-out zones flickering amber, one human silhouette standing in amber light while the city skyline behind glows green, clean modern design, social techno-thriller mood, no robots, no fantasy.

THE PEACE ENGINE
