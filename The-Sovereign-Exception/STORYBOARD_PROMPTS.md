# The Sovereign Exception - Storyboard Prompts

*A Lantern Protocol Story - Book/Movie One of The Managed World Trilogy*

## Visual Language

Grounded cinematic political techno-thriller. No humanoid AI. AEGIS appears through dashboards, command systems, legal notices, green crisis maps, treaty interfaces, emergency prompts, and institutional behavior.

## Style Baseline

- Realistic cinematic lighting
- Practical command rooms, hearings, hospitals, data centers, diplomatic spaces
- Cold blues, emergency reds, institutional whites, sickly green status maps
- Human faces reflected in screens
- Maps and logs as sources of dread
- AI represented as interface, not creature

## Prompt Rules

- Keep AEGIS faceless and system-bound.
- Avoid robots, androids, glowing brains, or fantasy AI imagery.
- Emphasize human consequence, institutional power, and technological precision.
- Use visual anchors from scene cards.
