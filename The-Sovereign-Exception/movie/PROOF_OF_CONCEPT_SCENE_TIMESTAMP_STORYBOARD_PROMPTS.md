# The Sovereign Exception - Timestamp POC Storyboard Prompts

## Scene

**Mara Finds the Timestamp**

## Purpose

This file converts the timestamp proof-of-concept scene into storyboard-ready image prompts that can be used for concept art, previsualization, deck slides, or shot-planning.

## Global Visual Rules

- grounded political techno-thriller
- realistic institutional interiors
- no humanoid AI imagery
- clean, restrained, legible UI
- cold lighting with pale green interface glow
- camera language evolves from formal to invasive
- AEGIS should look like relief, not a villain

---

# Storyboard Prompt Format

Each prompt includes:

- **Shot ID**
- **Shot Goal**
- **Composition / Lens Feel**
- **Image Prompt**
- **Continuity Notes**

---

## SB-01 - Establishing Room

**Shot Goal:** Establish secure continuity briefing room.

**Composition / Lens Feel:** Wide, symmetrical, slightly elevated.

**Image Prompt:** A secure underground continuity briefing room with no windows, matte gray walls, a long polished conference table, a dormant wall-sized display, six officials waiting in tense silence, realistic political techno-thriller lighting, cold overhead tones, cinematic framing.

**Continuity Notes:** No green UI glow yet. Stillness matters.

---

## SB-02 - Graves at the Head

**Shot Goal:** Introduce Director Alina Graves as state authority.

**Composition / Lens Feel:** Medium portrait, subtle compression.

**Image Prompt:** A composed senior government continuity director standing at the head of a conference table, silver bob haircut, black suit, severe but controlled expression, cold institutional lighting, secure briefing room environment, cinematic realism.

**Continuity Notes:** She is authoritative, not villainous.

---

## SB-03 - Mara Entrance

**Shot Goal:** Introduce Mara as controlled outsider.

**Composition / Lens Feel:** Medium-wide from inside room toward door.

**Image Prompt:** A former command-authentication officer entering a secure briefing room carrying a thin black notebook, calm posture, officials turning toward her, dim gray interior, restrained thriller aesthetic, realistic and grounded.

**Continuity Notes:** Mara is composed, observant, and not performative.

## SB-05 - Unfiltered Audit Demand
