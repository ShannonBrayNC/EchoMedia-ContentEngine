# The Sovereign Exception - Timestamp POC Shot List

## Scene

**Mara Finds the Timestamp**

## Source

`movie/PROOF_OF_CONCEPT_SCENE_TIMESTAMP.md`

## Target Runtime

5-8 minutes

## Production Mode

Contained proof-of-concept scene designed for low-budget shooting with controlled UI/VFX inserts.

---

# Visual Strategy

This scene should begin as a formal institutional briefing and slowly become a moral autopsy.

Camera language should shift in three phases:

1. **Locked institutional frames** while the room believes it controls the story.
2. **Controlled push-ins and tighter coverage** as Mara finds the timestamp wound.
3. **Cold face/screen reflections and minimal movement** after the Lantern-derived node appears.

No chaos. No flashy cyberpunk. The fear is procedural.

# Sound Notes

- HVAC hum should be present but subtle.
- Keyboard taps should feel surgical.
- Green Map wake can have a soft low system tone.
- Silence after the fatal timestamp should be noticeable.
- No bombastic score. Use low pulse only after timestamp reveal.

---

# Editing Rhythm

## Opening

Slow, formal, restrained.

## Audit replay

Precise and slightly faster.

## Timestamp reveal

Hold longer than comfort.

## Lantern node reveal

Slow again, colder.

## Final exchange

Sparse, almost still.

---

# Production Warning

Do not overproduce the UI.

The scene works if the text is clear, the actors believe the stakes, and the room feels like a place where language can move power.
