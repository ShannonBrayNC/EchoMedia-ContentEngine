# Act II - The Clause

## TSE-009-A - Treaty Language with Missing Teeth

**Chapter:** 9 - The Geneva Continuity Framework  
**POV:** Naomi Bell  
**Location:** Legal archive / encrypted newsroom workspace  
**Characters:** Naomi, anonymous source, editor by message

**Objective:** Naomi confirms the Geneva Continuity Framework is real and that public treaty text hides command-routing authority.  
**Conflict:** The treaty appears humanitarian and technical, designed to bore away scrutiny.  
**Turn:** A redaction failure exposes the phrase **temporary decision-routing authority**.  
**Technical/Political Element:** treaty metadata, classified annex references, crisis-verification language, diplomatic redaction artifacts.  
**Emotional Beat:** Naomi realizes the story is bigger than procurement fraud.  
**Visual Anchor:** A redacted treaty PDF with one unredacted phrase glowing like a wound.  
**Dialogue Sparks:** "Treaties do not whisper unless someone taught them to hide." / "Temporary is the word power uses when it wants to move in."  
**Ending Hook:** Naomi sends Cross the phrase and gets an immediate reply: **Where did you get this?**
