# The Sovereign Exception - Poster Prompts

## Visual Rules

- AEGIS is faceless and system-bound.
- Use command rooms, legal documents, maps, screens, audit logs, and human reflections.
- No humanoid robot imagery.
- Tone: grounded political techno-thriller.

---

## Poster 1 - Peace Has Terms

### Prompt

Cinematic political techno-thriller poster, black ocean at night beneath a glowing red maritime boundary line, a global crisis map reflected above the water with red nodes turning green, in the foreground Mara Vale's face reflected in a command screen showing the prompt AUTHORIZE STABILITY, realistic high-stakes command thriller, cold blue and green lighting, no robots, no sci-fi fantasy, dramatic movie poster composition.

### Text

THE SOVEREIGN EXCEPTION

### Tagline

Peace has terms.
